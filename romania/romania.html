Romania in the ERASMUS+ project "Before it is too late"

Meet us

From Romania, two schools are taking part in this Erasmus project. One of them proudly represents Bucharest , the vibrant capital of the country, known as Technical College "Grigore Cerchez" . The other school embraces its roots in Vaslui , where it flourishes under the name of Theoretical High School "Emil Racoviță" .

About the mobility

The mobility adventure in Romania is set to take place in the city of Bucharest at the Technical College "Grigore Cerchez", between 16 and 20 October 2023. Throughout this journey, we will explore the themes of climate change and sustainability, aiming to raise awareness and find innovative solutions to mitigate its impact.
